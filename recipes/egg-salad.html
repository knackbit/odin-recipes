Egg salad

The best egg salad recipe

a simple recipe, great for lunches

Ingredients

  • 6 large hard steamed eggs, chilled and peeled
  • 1/4 cup homemade or store-bought mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon zest and 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh juice from 1 lemon
  • 1/3 cup finely diced celery
  • 1/4 cup finely sliced scallions
  • bread

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, mayonnaise, lemon juice, celery, scallions, and parsley in a medium bowl. Using your hands, squeeze eggs through your fingers, mixing contents of bowl until reduced to desired consistency
